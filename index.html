Our Story

Tea as an art,
not a habit

Abija Tea was born from a single question: why does morning tea have to be ordinary? We sourced directly from highland estates in Sri Lanka's Uva and Nuwara Eliya regions — places where mist, altitude, and volcanic soil create something extraordinary.

The Cyclone collection is our answer. Each blend is small-batch, single-estate, and crafted with the same attention a winemaker gives a grand cru. No blending floors. No commodity tea. Just the leaf, the land, and the cup.
