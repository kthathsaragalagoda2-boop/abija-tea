Our Story

Tea as an art,
not a habit

Abija Tea is a Sri Lankan tea brand offering pure, unblended, single-estate Ceylon tea sourced directly from highland estates in the Uva and Nuwara Eliya regions — two of Sri Lanka's most celebrated tea-growing districts.

Each blend is small-batch and crafted with the same attention a winemaker gives a grand cru. No blending floors. No commodity tea. Just the leaf, the land, and the cup — with local delivery across Sri Lanka available on all orders.

  • ✦ Sourced directly from 3 partner estates in Sri Lanka's Uva & Nuwara Eliya highlands
  • ✦ All teas are 100% pure and unblended — no additives, no fillers
  • ✦ BOP (Broken Orange Pekoe) grade leaf — brisk, full-bodied, and amber
  • ✦ Available in English, Sinhala & Tamil — prices shown in GBP, LKR, and INR
  • ✦ Order via WhatsApp — local delivery across Sri Lanka
