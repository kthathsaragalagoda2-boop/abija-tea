Abija is a premium Sri Lankan tea brand built on purity, truth, and deep knowledge of real tea. Every product is sourced from Talawakelle, Kandy — 100% pure Ceylon tea, unblended, uncoloured, unflavoured, and free from chemicals or additives. Abija does not sell an image of tea. It sells tea itself.
